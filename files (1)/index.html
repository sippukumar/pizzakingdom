👑 Our Story

Born from Passion,
Perfected by Fire

Pizza Kingdom was founded in 1998 by Chef Marco Rossi in a small Naples-inspired kitchen in the heart of the city. Our wood-fired ovens reach 900°F — hotter than any conventional oven — creating a perfectly charred crust you simply can't replicate.

Every topping is sourced locally. Every sauce is hand-stirred. Every pizza is a promise.
